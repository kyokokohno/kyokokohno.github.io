I am a 22-year old pianist based in Lakeview, Chicago. I take gigs on the evenings and weekends for weddings, bars, events, and more.

What I can do for you

I do accompaniment, improv, and personal arrangements of all different genres.

I am classically trained in piano for over 16 years. My repertoire ranges from Bach to Ravel, Chopin to Rachmaninoff, Gershwin to Earl Wild.

I can listen to a piece I’ve never heard of and make my own improvised arrangement within 10 minutes.

Your regular performer cancelled last minute? No problem. I’ll be able to assist with any events in the Chicago area accessible via public transit.

My Background

  • Northwestern University alumni (BM Piano Performance ‘21)

  • Royal Conservatory of Music alumni ‘17

  • Winner of International Piano Competition for Young Musicians (Netherlands, 2016)

  • Over 10 years of preparatory and professional experience in chamber music, accompaniment, and solo performances

  • Originally from Tokyo, Japan
