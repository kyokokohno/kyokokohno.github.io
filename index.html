I am a 22-year old pianist based in Lakeview, Chicago. I take gigs on the weekends for weddings, bars, events, and more.

What I can do for you

I do improv, sight-reading, and arrangements of all different genres.

I am classically trained in piano for over 16 years. My repertoire ranges from Bach to Ravel, Chopin to Rachmaninoff, Gershwin to Earl Wild.

My secret power is that I can listen to a piece I’ve never heard of and make my own improvised arrangement within 10 minutes.

Your regular performer cancelled last minute? No problem. I’ll be able to assist with any events in the Chicago area accessible via public transit.
